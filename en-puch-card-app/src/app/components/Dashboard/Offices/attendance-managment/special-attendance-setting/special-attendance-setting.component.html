• Work shifts for more than 24 hours are currently not supported (check-in and check-out hours should only be recorded in one day)
  • The buoyancy field is from the beginning and the end to the minute to manage the overall buoyancy. (For example, if you set this float to 60 minutes and your staff enters the workplace after 40 minutes, 40 minutes will be automatically added to the departure time during the calculations so that the delay is not recorded for the user, and also overtime up to 40 minutes are not considered, but if there is a delay of more than 60 minutes, it is considered as normal and will be delayed)
  • You can define up to 25 work shifts.
